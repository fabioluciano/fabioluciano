## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Brazilian Solutions Architect based in Brasília, passionate about building and optimizing high-performance development ecosystems. With solid experience across various areas of the software development lifecycle, my primary focus is on designing and implementing robust, scalable solutions that empower teams to deliver value efficiently.

My expertise spans from **Cloud Architecture & FinOps** to **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, ensuring that infrastructure is not only resilient and secure but also cost-efficient. I am a strong advocate for **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, constantly seeking to optimize workflows and tools to maximize developer productivity and satisfaction. Furthermore, I possess deep knowledge in **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**, **DevSecOps & Security**, **Software Engineering**, **Kubernetes & Containers**, and **Observability & SRE**, integrating these practices to create reliable and easy-to-manage systems.

I am dedicated to transforming complex challenges into simplified solutions, fostering a culture of technical excellence and continuous innovation. My journey is marked by the relentless pursuit of technologies that drive efficiency and by collaboration on projects that positively impact the development community.
