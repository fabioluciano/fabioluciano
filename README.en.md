## 👋 About Me
I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer based in Brasília, Brazil. My passion lies in crafting development ecosystems that not only function flawlessly but also inspire and empower teams. With a holistic approach, I navigate between **Cloud Architecture & FinOps** to optimize resources and costs, and **Platform Engineering (IDP)** to build robust and intuitive infrastructures.

My expertise delves into transforming the developer experience, applying **Developer Experience (DevEx)** principles to streamline workflows and accelerate delivery. I'm an enthusiast of **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**, constantly seeking to automate and optimize pipelines, and I integrate **DevSecOps & Security** from conception to ensure resilient systems. With a strong background in **Software Engineering**, I enjoy diving into **Kubernetes & Containers** solutions for scalable orchestration and exploring the nuances of **Observability & SRE** to maintain performant and reliable systems. I maintain a portfolio of 43 personal repositories and curate over 3250 starred repositories, reflecting a broad exploration of 7664 unique topics.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests
Currently, I am deeply engaged in and exploring the following areas, always striving to enhance teams' ability to deliver value with excellence and security:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing scalable and resilient cloud infrastructures, with a focus on cost optimization and financial governance (FinOps) to maximize ROI in environments like AWS and Azure.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Creating environments and tools that make the development lifecycle more efficient, enjoyable, and productive, from initial setup to production deployment. My blog reflects this passion.
*   🔄 **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**: Implementing continuous integration and continuous delivery (CI/CD) practices with advanced automation, ensuring fast, secure, and high-quality releases. Exploring tools like ArgoCD and Flux for GitOps.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security into every stage of the development pipeline, from static code analysis to policy management with tools like Open Policy Agent (OPA) and secrets management (SOPS).
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Developing and maintaining Internal Developer Platforms (IDP) that abstract infrastructure complexity, allowing product teams to focus on their core deliverables.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Applying software design principles, architecture, and best practices to build robust, maintainable, and scalable systems, proficient in Go, Rust, Python, and TypeScript.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Orchestrating containerized workloads with Kubernetes, exploring tools like Helm and operators for efficient cluster automation and management.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing monitoring, logging, and tracing solutions to ensure complete system visibility and applying Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) principles to maintain reliability and performance.
