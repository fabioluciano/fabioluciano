## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer passionate about building robust and efficient development ecosystems. With solid experience in **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**, and **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, my focus is on empowering teams, optimizing workflows, and ensuring technology sustainably serves business objectives.

My professional journey is marked by an unceasing quest for solutions that enhance **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, integrating **DevSecOps & Security** principles from conception. I believe that excellence in **Software Engineering** translates into resilient and scalable systems, leveraging the power of **Kubernetes & Containers** and ensuring operational visibility through **Observability & SRE**. I am the creator of `tmux-powerkit`, a tmux status bar framework with hundreds of stars, demonstrating my commitment to developer productivity tools.

Beyond building, I love exploring the frontiers of technology, diving into new languages like Go and Rust, and contributing to the community. My blog is a space where I share insights on productivity, culture, and the human impact on software engineering, reflecting my passion for going beyond the code.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests
Currently, my energy is directed towards deepening and applying expertise in the following areas:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing scalable and resilient cloud infrastructures, with a focus on cost optimization and financial governance to maximize the value of cloud investments.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Crafting environments and tools that make the development process more fluid, productive, and satisfying, reducing friction and increasing engineer satisfaction.
*   🔄 **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**: Implementing end-to-end continuous integration and delivery pipelines, automating processes to accelerate the software lifecycle and ensure fast, reliable releases.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security practices into all phases of the development cycle, from design to operation, to build systems that are secure by design and by default.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Developing Internal Developer Platforms (IDPs) that abstract infrastructure complexity, offering self-service and standardized tooling to accelerate innovation.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Applying design principles, architecture, and coding standards to build high-quality, maintainable, and performant software.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Orchestrating and managing applications in containerized environments with Kubernetes, ensuring scalability, resilience, and portability.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing monitoring, tracing, and logging solutions to gain deep insights into system behavior, ensuring high availability and performance through Site Reliability Engineering practices.

I am also constantly attentive to innovations in code agent technologies and intelligent automation.
