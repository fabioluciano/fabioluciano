## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Cloud Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer with an unwavering passion for optimizing the developer experience. With a solid foundation in **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, my work spans from designing resilient infrastructures to ensuring teams can build and deliver value efficiently. I believe that **Developer Experience (DevEx)** is at the heart of productivity, and I am dedicated to creating environments where flow and feedback are priorities.

My professional journey is marked by the continuous exploration and implementation of **DevOps & Modern CI/CD** practices, guaranteeing robust and automated pipelines. I have a keen eye for **DevSecOps & Security**, integrating security principles at all stages of the development lifecycle. As an enthusiast of **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, I strive to build internal platforms that empower developers, abstracting complexities and accelerating delivery. My expertise in **Software Engineering** allows me to transition between system design and the implementation of high-quality code, especially with a focus on **Kubernetes & Containers** for orchestration and scalability. Finally, I am a strong advocate for **Observability & SRE**, ensuring that systems are not only performant but also transparent and reliable.

Having starred over 3300 repositories, I am constantly exploring new technologies and trends, as evidenced by my `tmux-powerkit` project, a tmux status bar framework that reflects my commitment to tools that enhance productivity. My contribution manifests both in high-level architecture and in the implementation of solutions that truly make a difference for engineering teams.

*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Orchestrating containerized applications for high availability and scalability, mastering the Kubernetes ecosystem and its tools.

Furthermore, I am deeply engaged in exploring **AI Agents and LLMs** for optimizing development workflows, with a growing interest in how these technologies can revolutionize platform engineering and developer experience.
