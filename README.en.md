## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, an experienced Solutions Architect with a passion for transforming the developer experience and building robust engineering platforms. With a solid background in software engineering, my expertise spans from **Cloud Architecture & FinOps** to implementing **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**, ensuring teams operate with maximum efficiency and security.

My approach focuses on optimizing **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, creating environments where workflows are intuitive and productive. I am an enthusiast of **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, dedicated to building the foundations that empower developers to innovate rapidly. I possess deep knowledge in **Kubernetes & Containers**, **DevSecOps & Security**, and **Observability & SRE**, ensuring that solutions are not only scalable and efficient but also secure and resilient. I am a polyglot in technologies, with strong proficiency in Go, Rust, Python, and TypeScript, and an active maintainer of open-source projects that enhance engineers' daily lives.

My passion for systems and tooling is reflected in over 40 personal repositories and hundreds of indirect contributions through reviews and discussions. I believe that technical excellence goes hand-in-hand with architectural clarity and a culture of continuous improvement, always seeking technological frontiers to solve complex challenges.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests
My professional journey and recent explorations are concentrated on the following areas:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing scalable and resilient cloud solutions, with a keen eye on cost optimization and financial governance in cloud environments.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Elevating developer productivity and satisfaction through tools, processes, and platforms that simplify the software development lifecycle.
*   🔄 **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**: Implementing end-to-end continuous integration and delivery pipelines, automating workflows, and fostering a culture of collaboration and agility.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security practices into all phases of development and operations, ensuring applications and infrastructures are secure by design.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Building and maintaining Internal Developer Platforms (IDPs) that empower teams by offering self-service tools and services.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Applying software design and architecture principles to build robust, maintainable, and high-performance systems.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Orchestrating microservices and containerized applications to ensure scalability, resilience, and portability across any environment.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing monitoring, logging, and tracing solutions to gain deep visibility into systems, and applying Site Reliability Engineering principles to ensure availability and performance.
