## 👋 About Me
I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect passionate about transforming complex challenges into efficient and scalable solutions. With a solid foundation in **Software Engineering**, I'm dedicated to building technological ecosystems that not only function but also empower developers and optimize operations.

My expertise spans several crucial areas for modern development: from designing robust systems with **Cloud Architecture & FinOps** to implementing **Modern DevOps & CI/CD** practices that accelerate delivery. I'm an enthusiast of **Kubernetes & Containers**, utilizing them as pillars for resilient and portable infrastructures. Furthermore, I have a strong commitment to **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, always seeking to simplify workflows and enhance team productivity.

As a Platform Engineer (IDP), I specialize in creating internal platforms that abstract infrastructure complexity, allowing developers to focus on code. My approach integrates security from the outset with **DevSecOps & Security** and ensures system health through **Observability & SRE**. I maintain open-source projects like the acclaimed [tmux-powerkit](https://github.com/fabioluciano/tmux-powerkit) and the [kubestronaut](https://github.com/fabioluciano/kubestronaut) guide, reflecting my commitment to the community and knowledge sharing.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests
Currently, my work and research focus on deepening and applying the following areas:
* ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing resilient infrastructures and optimizing costs in cloud environments, with a focus on providers like AWS and Azure.
* 👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Creating tools, processes, and environments that make development more productive, enjoyable, and efficient for engineering teams.
* 🔄 **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**: Implementing continuous integration and continuous delivery pipelines that automate the software development lifecycle, ensuring agility and quality.
* 🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security practices at all stages of the development cycle, from design to operation, to build inherently secure systems.
* 🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Developing and maintaining Internal Developer Platforms that simplify the consumption of infrastructure and services for developers.
* ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Applying design principles, architectural patterns, and best practices to build high-quality, scalable, and maintainable software.
* ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Orchestrating containerized applications with Kubernetes, exploring the latest trends in cluster management and workload optimization.
* 📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing monitoring, logging, and tracing solutions to ensure the health, performance, and reliability of production systems, aligned with Site Reliability Engineering principles.

## 🚀 Projects in Development
While my recent public commit activity is moderate, I am actively engaged in evolving projects that reflect my passions and expertise:
*   **[tmux-powerkit](https://github.com/fabioluciano/tmux-powerkit)** (Shell): A robust framework for creating custom tmux status bars, boasting over 500 stars. Continuously enhancing the experience for developers who rely on the terminal.
*   **[kubestronaut](https://github.com/fabioluciano/kubestronaut)**: A comprehensive and practical guide to mastering Kubernetes and preparing for certifications like KCNA and CKA. This reflects my commitment to democratizing knowledge in container orchestration.
*   **[fabioluciano/guides](https://github.com/fabioluciano/guides)** (TypeScript): A developing collection of guides and tutorials, covering software engineering topics and development practices, with a focus on clarity and applicability.
*   **[fabioluciano/dot](https://github.com/fabioluciano/dot)** (Lua): My dotfiles, a personal project focused on optimizing my development environment with custom configurations for tools like Neovim and tmux, showcasing my interest in Developer Experience.
