## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer passionate about building robust and efficient systems that empower developers. My professional journey is driven by a continuous pursuit of optimization, whether in cloud infrastructure, development experience, or application security. With a strong foundation in **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**, **DevSecOps & Security**, **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, **Software Engineering**, **Kubernetes & Containers**, and **Observability & SRE**, I operate at the intersection of cutting-edge technology and business value.

I believe that the key to success in software engineering lies in simplifying complex processes and creating environments where teams can innovate rapidly and securely. I am an open-source enthusiast, always exploring new tools and contributing to the community, which is reflected in my extensive collection of starred projects and my activity in reviews and discussions.

My passion for Developer Experience leads me to deeply investigate how teams interact with their tools and processes, tirelessly seeking ways to remove friction and improve workflows. This commitment to technical and operational excellence allows me to design and implement solutions that not only meet technical requirements but also drive developer productivity and satisfaction.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests
Currently, I am dedicated to deepening and applying knowledge in the following strategic areas:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing scalable, resilient, and secure cloud infrastructures, with a focus on cost optimization and financial governance (FinOps) to maximize ROI.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Crafting tools, platforms, and processes that simplify the development lifecycle, boosting engineer productivity and satisfaction.
*   🔄 **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**: Implementing automated and efficient Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery (CI/CD) pipelines, fostering a culture of collaboration and rapid delivery.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security practices throughout all phases of the development lifecycle, ensuring applications and infrastructures are secure from design to operation.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Developing and maintaining Internal Developer Platforms (IDPs) that provide self-service capabilities, tools, and automation for engineering teams.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Applying software design principles, architectural patterns, and best practices to build high-quality, maintainable, and scalable systems.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Orchestrating containerized applications with Kubernetes, designing microservices solutions, and managing clusters for high availability and scalability.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing monitoring, logging, and tracing solutions to gain deep insights into system behavior and ensure operational reliability (SRE).
