## 👋 About Me
I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer with a passion for building robust and efficient systems that empower development teams. Based in Brasília, Brazil, I dedicate myself to transforming complex challenges into scalable solutions, always with a keen eye on optimizing developer experience and operational sustainability.

My expertise spans a broad spectrum of modern engineering, including **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, where I design cost-effective infrastructures; **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, creating environments that maximize productivity; **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**, automating continuous deliveries; **DevSecOps & Security**, integrating security from design; **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, building internal developer platforms; **Software Engineering**, developing high-quality applications; **Kubernetes & Containers**, orchestrating microservices; and **Observability & SRE**, ensuring system reliability. I'm an enthusiast of technologies like Go, Rust, TypeScript, Python, and Shell Script, and an active maintainer of open-source projects, such as the popular `tmux-powerkit`.

I believe that technology should serve as a catalyst for innovation, and my work focuses on bridging the gap between strategic vision and technical execution, empowering developers and ensuring that software operations are resilient and efficient. My approach is practical, results-oriented, and deeply rooted in engineering culture and continuous improvement.
