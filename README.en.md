## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer with a passion for creating environments where developers can thrive. My professional journey is driven by a continuous pursuit of efficiency, security, and scalability in complex systems. With a solid foundation in **Software Engineering**, I am dedicated to transforming technical challenges into robust and innovative solutions.

My expertise ranges from cost and resource optimization with **Cloud Architecture & FinOps** to implementing cutting-edge practices in **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**. I am a strong advocate for **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, believing that an optimized workflow and effective tools are crucial for any team's success. Furthermore, I possess deep knowledge in **DevSecOps & Security**, ensuring that security is intrinsic to the development lifecycle.

I specialize in building and managing platforms through **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, with a particular focus on **Kubernetes & Containers** for orchestration and automation. My approach also extends to **Observability & SRE**, ensuring that systems not only function but do so reliably and predictably. I am always exploring new technologies to enhance how we build and deliver software.
