## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect passionate about building robust systems and optimizing the developer journey. My expertise spans from **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, ensuring efficient and economically viable infrastructures, to **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, creating environments that empower developers.

With a holistic view, I operate at the intersection of **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**, and **DevSecOps & Security**, focusing on automating, securing, and accelerating the software development lifecycle. I am an enthusiast of **Kubernetes & Containers**, using them to orchestrate scalable applications, and a proponent of **Observability & SRE** to maintain resilient systems. My passion for optimizing development environments is reflected in personal projects and the continuous pursuit of improving the productivity and satisfaction of engineering teams.

With over 50 public repositories and extensive exploration of technologies, I constantly seek to expand my knowledge in **Software Engineering**, always with the goal of delivering high-impact solutions. I am driven by curiosity and the desire to share learnings, as evidenced by my `kubestronaut` guide and the articles on my blog.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests
Currently, I am deeply engaged in and exploring the following areas:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing and optimizing scalable and cost-effective infrastructures in cloud environments, with a focus on AWS and GCP, and implementing financial management practices to maximize cloud value.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Creating tools, processes, and platforms that make developers' lives more productive, joyful, and efficient, removing friction and streamlining workflows.
*   🔄 **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**: Implementing continuous delivery pipelines and advanced DevOps practices for automation, agility, and reliability throughout the entire development lifecycle.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security into all phases of the development lifecycle, from design to operation, with a focus on IaC Security, Supply Chain Security, and automated security testing.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Developing and maintaining internal developer platforms that abstract infrastructure complexity, providing self-service tools and standardized environments.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Applying software design principles, clean architecture, code standards, and refactoring to build robust, maintainable, and high-performance applications.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Orchestrating microservices and managing containerized workloads for high availability, scalability, and portability across various environments.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing monitoring, logging, and tracing solutions to ensure system health and performance, following Site Reliability Engineering principles for resilience and efficient operation.
