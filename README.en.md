## 👋 About Me
I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect with a deep passion for transforming the developer experience and optimizing cloud-native ecosystems. My professional journey has led me to master crucial areas such as Cloud Architecture, FinOps, Developer Experience (DevEx), DevOps, DevSecOps, Platform Engineering (IDP), Software Engineering, Kubernetes & Containers, and Observability & SRE. I believe that excellence in software engineering is not limited to code alone, but extends to creating environments where developers thrive, costs are optimized, and security is intrinsic.

With a portfolio spanning languages like Go, Rust, Python, and TypeScript, and a dedication to open source evidenced by projects like `tmux-powerkit`, I am always exploring the frontiers of technology. My curiosity drives me to constantly learn and integrate new tools and concepts, with a recent and growing focus on Artificial Intelligence and intelligent automation, to build more efficient and impactful solutions.

As an active thinker and writer, I share my insights and discoveries on my blog, contributing to discussions on productivity, workflow, and the challenges of modern engineering. My goal is not only to build robust systems but also to inspire and empower other professionals to elevate their standards and practices.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests
My passion for innovation and optimization leads me to delve into the following areas, where I constantly seek to improve my knowledge and apply best practices:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing and implementing resilient, scalable, and cost-effective architectures in public cloud environments, with a focus on cost optimization and financial governance.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Creating environments and tools that empower developers to be more productive, reducing friction and improving the workflow from start to finish.
*   🔄 **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**: Automating and streamlining the software development lifecycle, from continuous integration to delivery and deployment, with an emphasis on efficient pipelines and a culture of collaboration.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security practices into all phases of the development lifecycle, ensuring that security is a concern from design to operation.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Building and maintaining internal development platforms that abstract infrastructure complexity, allowing engineering teams to focus on delivering value.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Applying robust design principles, architectural patterns, and best coding practices to build high-quality, maintainable, and scalable software.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Orchestrating and managing containerized applications at scale, mastering Kubernetes ecosystems for deployment, scalability, and resilience.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing monitoring, logging, and tracing solutions to ensure system health and performance, and applying Site Reliability Engineering principles for high availability.
