## 👋 About Me

I'm Fábio Luciano, a Cloud Solutions Architect with a profound passion for transforming how engineering teams build and deliver software. My career has focused on optimizing development and production environments, specializing in designing and implementing resilient and efficient infrastructures that empower developers. My work spans from **Cloud Architecture & FinOps** to creating robust **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, always with a keen eye on **Developer Experience (DevEx)**.

My expertise extends across a vast technological landscape, including modernizing **DevOps & Modern CI/CD** practices, integrating security at all stages with **DevSecOps & Security**, and the art of **Software Engineering** to build scalable systems. I am an enthusiast of **Kubernetes & Containers**, leveraging them to orchestrate complex applications, and a proponent of **Observability & SRE** to ensure continuous reliability and performance. I believe technology should serve to amplify human capability, and my focus is on building bridges between technical complexity and the fluidity of a developer's work.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests

Currently, I am deeply immersed in several key areas that are shaping the future of software development and infrastructure:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing scalable, resilient, and cost-efficient solutions in cloud environments, with a focus on cost optimization and governance.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Crafting tools, processes, and platforms that empower developers, making the software development lifecycle more productive and enjoyable.
*   🔄 **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**: Implementing continuous integration and continuous delivery pipelines that automate and accelerate the release of high-quality software.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security practices from design to operation, ensuring security is an intrinsic part of the development process.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Building internal developer platforms that abstract infrastructure complexity, allowing development teams to focus on business logic.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Developing robust and high-performance systems, applying design and architectural principles to ensure maintainability and scalability.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Orchestrating containerized applications for flexible, scalable, and highly available deployments, exploring the latest ecosystem features.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing monitoring, logging, and tracing solutions to gain deep insights into system behavior and ensure operational reliability.

## 🚀 Projects in Development

Built in Shell, it's a testament to my commitment to tools that enhance Developer Experience.
