## 👋 About Me
Hi there! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer passionate about building scalable systems and optimizing the developer experience. With a solid foundation in **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, **Software Engineering**, and **Kubernetes & Containers**, I'm dedicated to creating robust solutions that not only work but also empower engineering teams.

My professional journey is marked by a relentless pursuit of efficiency and innovation. I possess expertise in **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**, ensuring fast and reliable deliveries, and in **DevSecOps & Security**, integrating security practices from the early stages of development. I believe that an exceptional **Developer Experience (DevEx)** is key to high-performing teams, which is why I actively engage in **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, building tools and infrastructure that simplify developers' lives.

With a keen eye for **Observability & SRE**, I ensure systems are resilient and performant. My experience with Go, Rust, and TypeScript, coupled with strong involvement in the open-source community (evidenced by projects like `tmux-powerkit` and over 3000 repository stars), reflects my commitment to continuous learning and technical excellence.
