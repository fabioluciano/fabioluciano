## 👋 About Me

I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer passionate about building robust, scalable, and efficient systems. My professional journey focuses on transforming how teams develop and deliver software, applying a strategic vision across **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**, **DevSecOps & Security**, **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, **Software Engineering**, **Kubernetes & Containers**, and **Observability & SRE**.

With solid experience, I dedicate myself to designing and implementing solutions that not only meet technical requirements but also optimize developer workflows and ensure the security and resilience of operations. I believe that technical excellence must go hand-in-hand with a culture of innovation and continuous improvement.

I am an enthusiast for knowledge sharing, actively contributing to the community through open-source projects, articles on my blog, and engagement in technical discussions. My curiosity drives me to constantly explore new technologies and trends, especially in developer tooling and artificial intelligence applied to development.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests

My current work and research are concentrated on:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps:** Optimizing cloud infrastructure design for maximum efficiency, scalability, and cost control.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx):** Designing and implementing tools and processes that make the software development lifecycle more productive and enjoyable.
*   🔄 **Modern DevOps & CI/CD:** Automating and enhancing continuous integration and continuous delivery pipelines to accelerate time-to-market.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security:** Integrating security practices into all phases of the development lifecycle, from design to operation.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP):** Building and maintaining Internal Developer Platforms that empower teams to deliver value autonomously and securely.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering:** Applying design and coding principles to develop high-quality, maintainable, and scalable software.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers:** Mastering container orchestration to build and manage resilient cloud-native applications.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE:** Implementing monitoring systems and Site Reliability Engineering practices to ensure the health and performance of applications.
