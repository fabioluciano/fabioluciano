## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests
Currently, my main focus areas and interests include:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing and implementing scalable, resilient, and cost-optimized cloud architectures, with a focus on governance and financial efficiency in multi-cloud environments.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Crafting environments and workflows that make a developer's life easier, more productive, and enjoyable, through automation, intuitive tooling, and clear documentation.
*   🔄 **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**: Implementing end-to-end Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery (CI/CD) pipelines, utilizing tools like Tekton and Argo, for automation, speed, and reliability in the software lifecycle.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security practices into all phases of development, from conception to operation, ensuring compliance and protection of applications and infrastructure.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Developing and managing Internal Developer Platforms (IDPs) that abstract infrastructure complexity, providing self-service tools and services for engineering teams.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Focusing on distributed systems design, microservices, and developing high-quality, performant, and maintainable software, using languages like Go and Rust.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Orchestrating and managing containerized applications at scale, exploring ecosystems like KubeVPN for cloud-native development environments.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing monitoring, tracing, and logging solutions (e.g., Prometheus, Grafana, OpenTelemetry) to ensure visibility, reliability, and performance of complex systems.
