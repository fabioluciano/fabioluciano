## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer passionate about building scalable, resilient, and efficient systems. With solid expertise ranging from **Cloud Architecture & FinOps** to **Software Engineering** and **Observability & SRE**, I am dedicated to optimizing **Developer Experience (DevEx)** and implementing modern **DevOps & CI/CD** practices, with a keen eye on **DevSecOps & Security**.

My professional journey is marked by continuous exploration and mastery of cutting-edge technologies, especially within the **Kubernetes & Containers** ecosystem. I am an Open Source enthusiast, with notable contributions like `tmux-powerkit`, and an active observer of technological trends, evidenced by over 3300 starred repositories and a vast array of explored topics.

I believe that **Platform Engineering (IDP)** is the future for high-performance development teams, and I am always seeking ways to enhance software delivery, making it more secure, faster, and more enjoyable for developers.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests
Currently, my focus and passion lie in the following areas, where I aim to deepen my knowledge and apply innovative solutions:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing and optimizing cloud infrastructures for maximum scalability, resilience, and cost efficiency, ensuring the financial sustainability of operations.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Creating environments, tools, and processes that empower developers to be more productive, reducing friction and enhancing satisfaction in the workflow.
*   🔄 **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**: Implementing continuous delivery and continuous integration pipelines that automate, accelerate, and ensure software quality and security from commit to production.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security practices intrinsically into the development lifecycle, from design to operation, ensuring security is a fundamental pillar rather than an afterthought.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Building internal developer platforms that abstract infrastructure complexity, providing self-service and standardized tools for teams.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Developing robust, high-performance, and high-quality systems, with a focus on clean design, sustainable architectural patterns, and efficient code engineering.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Orchestrating and managing containerized applications at scale, using Kubernetes as the backbone of modern, resilient production environments.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing comprehensive monitoring, logging, tracing, and metrics solutions to ensure the reliability, performance, and health of systems in production, facilitating proactive problem resolution.
