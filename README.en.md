## 👋 About Me

Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect passionate about building robust and efficient systems that drive innovation. Based in Brasília, Brazil, I dedicate myself to shaping cloud-native architecture strategies and optimizing the developer experience for engineering teams. My professional journey is marked by a relentless pursuit of solutions that not only solve complex technical challenges but also empower developers to be more productive and satisfied.

My expertise spans a broad and critical spectrum for today's technological landscape, including **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**, **DevSecOps & Security**, **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, **Software Engineering**, **Kubernetes & Containers**, and **Observability & SRE**. I believe that combining strategic vision with technical execution is fundamental to delivering real and sustainable value. I am an open-source enthusiast, actively contributing and sharing knowledge through projects like `tmux-powerkit` and the `kubestronaut` guide.

With 56 public repositories and extensive exploration of technologies, I am always at the forefront, investigating new tools and methodologies to enhance how we build and deliver software. My passion lies in transforming complex ideas into practical, scalable, and secure solutions, always with a keen eye on the experience of those who build and use them.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests

My main areas of work and research include:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing and implementing resilient, scalable, and cost-optimized cloud infrastructures, with a focus on governance and financial efficiency.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Creating environments, tools, and processes that simplify developers' workflows, reducing friction and maximizing productivity and satisfaction.
*   🔄 **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**: Full automation of the software development lifecycle, from continuous integration (CI) to continuous delivery (CD), ensuring agility and reliability.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security practices into all phases of development, implementing automation and a security-first culture to protect applications and infrastructure.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Building and maintaining internal developer platforms that abstract infrastructure complexity, allowing product teams to focus on business logic.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Developing high-performance, scalable, and maintainable systems, applying robust design principles and quality code standards.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Advanced container orchestration and management, designing and implementing cloud-native solutions that leverage Kubernetes to its fullest potential.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing proactive monitoring systems, centralized logging, and distributed tracing to ensure the reliability, performance, and resilience of services.

*   **[fabioluciano/tmux-powerkit](https://github.com/fabioluciano/tmux-powerkit)**: A robust framework for the tmux status bar, focused on customization and terminal productivity, boasting over 500 stars. Developed in Shell, it showcases my passion for CLI tools and workflow optimization.
