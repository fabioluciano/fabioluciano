## 👋 About Me
I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer with a deep passion for optimizing the development experience and building robust, scalable infrastructures. My professional journey is marked by a constant pursuit of excellence in **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, and **Platform Engineering (IDP)**. I believe that the combination of intelligent automation, robust security, and a frictionless developer workflow is key to high-performing engineering teams.

With consolidated expertise in **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**, **DevSecOps & Security**, and **Software Engineering**, I focus on designing and implementing solutions that not only meet technical requirements but also drive productivity and innovation. I am an enthusiast of **Kubernetes & Containers**, actively exploring how these technologies can be leveraged to create resilient and efficient environments, always with a keen eye on **Observability & SRE** to ensure continuous reliability and performance of systems. My approach is polyglot, with strong proficiency in Go, Rust, TypeScript, and Python, and a proven track record of significant contributions to open-source projects.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests
My curiosity and experience drive me to continuously explore and deepen my knowledge in the following areas, always seeking best practices and innovations:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing scalable, resilient, and cost-optimized cloud infrastructures, with a focus on FinOps strategies to maximize the value of cloud investments.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Creating environments and tools that make the development lifecycle more fluid, productive, and enjoyable, from writing code to deploying to production.
*   🔄 **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**: Implementing automated and efficient CI/CD pipelines, promoting a DevOps culture for fast, reliable, and continuous deliveries.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security practices at all stages of the development cycle, from conception to operation, ensuring the protection of applications and infrastructures.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Developing and maintaining internal developer platforms (IDPs) that abstract infrastructure complexity, empowering teams to focus on business logic.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Applying principles of robust software design and architectural patterns to build high-quality, maintainable, and extensible systems.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Orchestrating and managing containerized applications at scale, exploring advanced Kubernetes capabilities for resilience and automation.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing monitoring, logging, and tracing solutions to ensure complete visibility into systems, applying Site Reliability Engineering principles for high availability.
