## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer based in Brasília, Brazil. My professional journey is driven by the relentless pursuit of optimization and innovation throughout the software development lifecycle. With a holistic view, I am dedicated to building robust, scalable, and efficient systems, always focusing on enhancing the experience of those who build and use them.

My expertise deeply covers **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, ensuring not only the scalability and resilience of infrastructures but also intelligent cost management. I am a strong advocate for **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, constantly striving to simplify and improve developers' workflows to maximize productivity and satisfaction. I have extensive experience in implementing **Modern DevOps & CI/CD** for continuous and secure deliveries, and I integrate **DevSecOps & Security** from the early design phases. My work extends to **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, where I build infrastructures that empower development teams. As a **Software Engineer**, I focus on high-quality solutions, with deep knowledge in **Kubernetes & Containers** and **Observability & SRE** systems to ensure application reliability and performance.

I am the maintainer of notable projects such as `tmux-powerkit`, a widely adopted framework for the tmux status bar, and `kubestronaut`, a comprehensive guide for Kubernetes certifications. Through my blog, I share insights on DevEx and productivity, reflecting my commitment to continuous improvement and collaboration within the tech community.
