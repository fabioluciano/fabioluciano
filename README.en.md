## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer with a deep passion for transforming how development teams operate. My professional journey is driven by a continuous pursuit of efficiency, security, and an impeccable Developer Experience (DevEx). I specialize in designing and implementing robust solutions in **Cloud Architecture & FinOps** environments, ensuring not only scalability but also cost and resource optimization.

Throughout my career, I've delved deeply into **DevOps & Modern CI/CD** and **DevSecOps & Security**, integrating security practices from the early stages of the development lifecycle. I have a strong background in **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, building tools and platforms that empower developers to deliver value faster. My expertise in **Software Engineering** is complemented by a practical mastery of **Kubernetes & Containers**, orchestrating complex applications with resilience and high availability. Furthermore, I am an enthusiast of **Observability & SRE**, creating systems that not only function but are also understandable and sustainable.

I believe that technology should amplify human potential, which is why I'm dedicated to creating environments where developers can thrive. Currently, I'm exploring the frontiers of artificial intelligence and how it can further enhance the engineering workflow.
