## 👋 About Me
I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer passionate about optimizing developer experience and building robust, efficient infrastructures. With a solid foundation in **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**, and **Software Engineering**, I dedicate myself to designing scalable and resilient systems. My expertise extends to orchestrating complex environments with **Kubernetes & Containers**, ensuring operations are fluid and secure.

My professional journey is driven by the relentless pursuit of improvements in **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, creating environments where engineering teams can thrive and innovate. This is complemented by a strong commitment to **DevSecOps & Security**, integrating security practices from the early stages of the development cycle. Furthermore, I'm an enthusiast of **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, aiming to simplify software delivery through well-designed internal platforms.

I believe that observability is key to healthy systems, which is why I have a continuous focus on **Observability & SRE**, implementing solutions that provide deep insights into application performance and health. I am an active open-source project maintainer and a continuous learner, always exploring new technological frontiers, especially in artificial intelligence and developer productivity tools.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests
Currently, my energy is concentrated on deepening and applying knowledge in the following areas, always striving for excellence and innovation:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing scalable and resilient cloud infrastructures, with a keen eye on cost optimization and operational efficiency.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Creating environments and tools that empower developers, reducing friction and enhancing team productivity and satisfaction.
*   🔄 **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**: Implementing fast, reliable, and automated continuous integration and delivery pipelines, accelerating the software development lifecycle.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security at every stage of development and operations, ensuring applications and infrastructures are protected by design.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Building and refining internal development platforms that abstract infrastructure complexity, allowing teams to focus on business logic.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Developing high-quality software, with a focus on clean design, performance, maintainability, and scalability.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Mastering container orchestration to efficiently and robustly deploy, scale, and manage applications.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing monitoring, logging, and tracing systems to ensure the health, availability, and performance of critical systems, applying Site Reliability Engineering principles.
