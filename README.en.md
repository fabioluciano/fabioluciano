## 👋 About Me

I'm Fábio Luciano, a Cloud Solutions Architect with a deep passion for optimizing the developer experience and building robust, efficient infrastructures. With 54 public repositories and a community of 141 followers, my work reflects a continuous commitment to innovation and knowledge sharing within the technology ecosystem. My expertise spans from **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, ensuring solutions are not only scalable but also economically viable, to implementing **Modern DevOps & CI/CD** to accelerate the software development lifecycle.

My professional journey is marked by an unceasing pursuit of excellence in **Software Engineering**, with a particular focus on **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, where I craft the tools and environments that empower other teams to thrive. I believe that **Developer Experience (DevEx)** is key to high-performing teams, and I am dedicated to eliminating friction and fostering an intuitive workflow. Furthermore, I am proficient in **Kubernetes & Containers**, orchestrating complex systems, and a strong advocate for **DevSecOps & Security**, integrating security practices from conception to operation. **Observability & SRE** complete my skill set, ensuring systems are not only built but also maintained with resilience and visibility.

My contributions extend beyond code, as evidenced by my recent articles, which explore the intersection of productivity, workflow, and corporate culture. While my public commit activity may have been "light" over the past 30 days, I remain actively engaged through Pull Request reviews and issue discussions, constantly exploring new technological frontiers, especially in languages like Rust, Go, and TypeScript.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests

Currently, my main areas of expertise and interest include:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Developing strategies to design, deploy, and manage cloud infrastructures efficiently, with a keen eye on cost optimization and financial sustainability.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Focused on enhancing the environment and tools that directly impact developers' productivity, satisfaction, and effectiveness, creating a smoother and more enjoyable workflow.
*   🔄 **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**: Implementing and optimizing Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery (CI/CD) pipelines, automating processes, and ensuring the agility and reliability of software deliveries.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security practices at all stages of the development lifecycle, from planning to operation, ensuring security is a fundamental pillar, not an afterthought.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Building and maintaining Internal Developer Platforms that provide standardized tools, services, and infrastructure, empowering development teams to operate with greater autonomy and efficiency.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Applying principles of system design, development, and maintenance to build robust, scalable, and high-quality solutions that meet business requirements.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Specialized in the orchestration and management of containerized applications in production environments, leveraging Kubernetes to ensure scalability, resilience, and portability.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing monitoring, logging, and tracing systems to gain deep insights into application behavior, and applying Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) principles to ensure service reliability and availability.
