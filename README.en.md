## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests
My passion lies at the intersection of infrastructure, development, and the end-user experience (the developer). Currently, my main areas of focus and interest include:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing scalable, resilient, and cost-optimized cloud infrastructures, with a keen eye on financial governance and operational efficiency.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Creating environments, tools, and processes that empower developers to be more productive, happy, and effective, reducing friction and increasing workflow.
*   🔄 **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**: Implementing end-to-end automation practices and pipelines, from continuous integration to continuous delivery and deployment, aiming for agility and reliability.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security into all phases of the software development lifecycle, automating checks and ensuring that security is an inherent aspect of design.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Building and maintaining internal developer platforms that abstract infrastructure complexity, offering self-service capabilities and accelerating time-to-value for teams.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Applying robust software design principles, architectural patterns, and best coding practices to build high-quality, sustainable applications.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Orchestrating workloads in cloud-native environments, managing containers, and exploring the full potential of the Kubernetes ecosystem for scalability and resilience.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing monitoring, logging, and tracing solutions to gain deep visibility into systems, and applying Site Reliability Engineering principles to ensure reliability and performance.
