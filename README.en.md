## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Cloud Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer with a passion for building robust, scalable, and secure systems in cloud environments. My professional journey is driven by a continuous pursuit of optimization and innovation, focusing on delivering value through infrastructure and development solutions.

With a solid foundation in **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**, **DevSecOps & Security**, **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, **Software Engineering**, **Kubernetes & Containers**, and **Observability & SRE**, I am dedicated to creating environments that empower development teams to operate with maximum efficiency and security. I am an enthusiast of technologies like Go, Rust, and TypeScript, using them to forge cutting-edge solutions.

I believe that technical excellence goes hand-in-hand with a culture of collaboration and continuous improvement. My experience allows me to transition from architectural conception to practical implementation, always with a keen eye on performance, cost, and developer experience.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests
Currently, my efforts and studies are concentrated on:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing resilient infrastructures and optimizing costs in cloud environments, ensuring efficiency and scalability.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Crafting tools and processes that make developers' lives easier and more productive, from development environment to deployment.
*   🔄 **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**: Implementing continuous integration and delivery pipelines that automate and accelerate the software development lifecycle, focusing on agility and reliability.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security practices into all phases of development and operations, protecting applications and infrastructure against threats.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Building and maintaining internal developer platforms that abstract infrastructure complexity, allowing teams to focus on core code.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Developing high-quality software, with a focus on clean design, performance, and maintainability, using languages like Go and Rust.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Orchestrating applications at scale with Kubernetes and exploring the container ecosystem for efficient and portable deployments.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing monitoring, logging, and tracing solutions to ensure system health and performance, adhering to Site Reliability Engineering principles.
