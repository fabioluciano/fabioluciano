With a solid career as a Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer, Fábio Luciano is dedicated to building robust and efficient development ecosystems. His expertise deeply covers **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**, **DevSecOps & Security**, **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, **Software Engineering**, **Kubernetes & Containers**, and **Observability & SRE**. His insights into Developer Experience are frequently shared on his blog, solidifying his position as a thought leader in the field.

## 🚀 Projects in Development
Currently, I am dedicated to projects that enhance developer productivity and infrastructure:

*   **[tmux-powerkit](https://github.com/fabioluciano/tmux-powerkit)** (Shell): A robust and highly customizable framework for tmux status bars, used by hundreds of developers to optimize their terminal environment.
*   **[tekton-events-relay](https://github.com/fabioluciano/tekton-events-relay)** (Go): A production-ready CloudEvents bridge that transforms Tekton pipeline events into notifications and automations, crucial for CI/CD observability and automation.
*   **[dot](https://github.com/fabioluciano/dot)** (Go Template): My dotfiles and environment configurations, maintained as a template for agility and consistency across different machines and development contexts.
*   Exploring AI tools and agents for engineering workflow optimization, with a focus on models like Claude and integration with platforms such as OpenCode.
