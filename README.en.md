## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect passionate about building robust, scalable, and secure systems. My professional journey is focused on transforming how teams develop and deliver software, optimizing infrastructures, and fostering a culture of operational excellence and security.

With solid expertise in **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, and **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, I am dedicated to creating environments where innovation thrives. I am a strong advocate for modern **DevOps & CI/CD** and **DevSecOps & Security** practices, ensuring security is intrinsic to the development lifecycle. My expertise extends to **Kubernetes & Containers** orchestration, high-quality **Software Engineering**, and **Observability & SRE** to maintain resilient systems.

I am a continuous learning enthusiast, always exploring new technologies and contributing to the open-source community, notably with the `tmux-powerkit` project. My goal is to simplify complexities, automate processes, and empower developers to focus on what they do best: creating value.
