## 👋 About Me
With a passion for architecting resilient solutions and optimizing the developer experience, I am a **Solutions Architect** with a solid foundation in **Platform Engineering**. My professional journey is marked by the continuous exploration of technologies that drive efficiency and security, ranging from **Cloud Architecture & FinOps** to **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**, with a keen eye on **DevSecOps & Security**.

I am a **Kubernetes & Containers** enthusiast, dedicated to building ecosystems that simplify developers' lives, optimize **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, and ensure **Observability & SRE**. My versatility is reflected in over 40 personal repositories and a curiosity that leads me to explore thousands of topics, always seeking to enhance **Software Engineering** and contribute to the community, as evidenced by the success of my `tmux-powerkit` project.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests
My attention is directed towards refining and exploring the following areas:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing scalable and resilient cloud infrastructures, with an emphasis on cost optimization and governance in Cloud Native environments, especially AWS.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Building tools and platforms that empower developers, simplifying workflows and increasing productivity through IDPs and CLIs.
*   🔄 **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**: Implementing automated pipelines and continuous delivery practices to accelerate the software development lifecycle and ensure agility.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security into all phases of the SDLC, from architecture to operation, with a focus on policy-as-code (Open Policy Agent) and secret management (SOPS).
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Developing and maintaining internal developer platforms to abstract infrastructure complexity and accelerate value delivery.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Designing and developing robust, performant, and high-quality systems, exploring languages like Go, Rust, Python, and TypeScript.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Orchestrating and managing applications in containerized environments, exploring tools like ArgoCD, Flux, and Kubernetes operators.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing monitoring, logging, and tracing solutions with Prometheus and Grafana to ensure the health, performance, and reliability of distributed systems.
