## 👋 About Me
I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect with a passion for optimizing Developer Experience (DevEx) and building robust, efficient platforms. With a solid foundation in **Cloud Architecture & FinOps** and **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, I am dedicated to creating environments that empower teams to deliver value in an agile, secure, and sustainable manner. My professional journey spans from modernizing **DevOps & Modern CI/CD** pipelines and implementing **DevSecOps & Security** practices, to orchestrating complex systems with **Kubernetes & Containers** and ensuring resilience through **Observability & SRE**.

My expertise extends beyond theory; I am a software engineer who values practical application and innovation. This is reflected in projects like `tmux-powerkit`, a tool that enhances terminal productivity, and in my frequent explorations of new technologies. With a vast collection of personal repositories and over 3000 starred repositories, I consistently stay updated and engaged with the evolving development and operations ecosystem.
