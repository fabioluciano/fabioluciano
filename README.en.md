## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect with an unwavering passion for building robust, scalable, and efficient cloud systems. With a deep specialization in Platform Engineering, I am dedicated to creating the foundations that empower development teams to deliver value quickly and securely. My expertise spans **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, where I not only design cutting-edge infrastructures but also optimize them for cost efficiency and financial governance.

My vision extends to **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, as I believe that a productive and enjoyable work environment is fundamental for innovation. I am a strong advocate for **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**, implementing practices and pipelines that automate and accelerate the software development lifecycle. Integrating security from the outset, my solutions incorporate **DevSecOps & Security** to build resilient and protected systems. Through **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, I build internal developer platforms that abstract infrastructure complexity, utilizing **Kubernetes & Containers** as pillars for application orchestration and management. Finally, I ensure system stability and performance with **Observability & SRE**, implementing proactive monitoring and reliability practices.

With a portfolio of 58 public repositories and a vast exploration of 3270 starred repositories and 7690 unique topics, my curiosity is the driving force behind my continuous learning. I am the maintainer of the popular open-source project `tmux-powerkit` and am always seeking to apply and share knowledge in languages like Go, Rust, Python, and TypeScript to solve complex challenges.
