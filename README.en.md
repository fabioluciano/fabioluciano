## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect passionate about building robust systems and optimizing the developer journey. With solid expertise spanning **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**, **DevSecOps & Security**, **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, **Software Engineering**, **Kubernetes & Containers**, and **Observability & SRE**, I'm dedicated to creating environments that not only function but empower teams to deliver value efficiently and securely.

My passion lies in demystifying infrastructure and development complexity, focusing on solutions that scale, are secure, and, above all, provide an exceptional developer experience. I'm an enthusiast of technologies like Go, Rust, and TypeScript, and I'm always exploring new approaches to optimize processes and infrastructures. Currently based in Brasília, Brazil, I contribute to the open-source community and share insights through my blog.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests
Currently, I am deeply involved in and actively exploring the following areas to drive innovation and efficiency:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing and optimizing cloud infrastructures for maximum performance and cost-effectiveness, with a focus on cloud financial management and resource governance.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Crafting tools, platforms, and processes that make developers' lives easier, more productive, and enjoyable, reducing friction and enhancing workflow.
*   🔄 **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**: Implementing continuous integration and continuous delivery pipelines that automate and accelerate the software development lifecycle, ensuring quality, security, and speed of delivery.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security practices at every stage of the development cycle, from design to operation, to build inherently secure and resilient systems.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Developing and maintaining Internal Developer Platforms (IDP) that abstract infrastructure complexity and provide self-service and standardized tools for developers.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Applying design and architectural principles to build scalable, maintainable, and performant software, with a focus on clean code, best practices, and design patterns.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Orchestrating applications in containerized environments for high availability, scalability, and portability, mastering the Kubernetes ecosystem and its tools.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing monitoring, logging, and tracing solutions to ensure system health and performance, and applying Site Reliability Engineering principles for resilient and efficient operations.
