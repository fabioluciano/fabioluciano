## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer passionate about building robust, scalable, and, above all, developer-friendly systems. Based in Brasília, Brazil, I am dedicated to optimizing the development journey, transforming complex challenges into efficient and impactful solutions. My experience spans from designing intricate architectures to implementing practices that enhance the productivity and satisfaction of engineering teams.

With a solid foundation in **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**, and **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, I am passionate about optimizing Developer Experience (DevEx). My work extends to **Software Engineering**, with a strong emphasis on **Kubernetes & Containers**, **Observability & SRE**, and ensuring security from design with **DevSecOps & Security**. I believe that technical excellence must go hand-in-hand with ease of use and collaboration.

As an open-source project maintainer and author of a blog focused on Developer Experience, I am always exploring new technologies and sharing insights to strengthen the community and drive innovation. My passion lies in creating environments where developers can focus on what they do best: building amazing software.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests
Currently, my main focuses and areas of interest include:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Optimizing costs and resources in multi-cloud environments, ensuring scalability, resilience, and financial efficiency.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Building platforms and tools that simplify developers' workflows, reducing friction, automating repetitive tasks, and increasing satisfaction.
*   🔄 **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**: Implementing end-to-end automated pipelines, from continuous integration to continuous delivery and deployment, accelerating the software development lifecycle with quality.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security as an intrinsic part of the development cycle, automating vulnerability scans, and ensuring compliance from the earliest stages.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Designing and implementing Internal Developer Platforms to standardize environments, abstract infrastructure complexities, and accelerate application development.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Developing robust and high-performing solutions, with a focus on clean code, resilient architectures, and scalable design patterns.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Orchestrating and managing containerized applications at scale, utilizing the Kubernetes ecosystem for automation and high availability.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing proactive monitoring, centralized logging, and distributed tracing to ensure the reliability, performance, and debuggability of distributed systems.
