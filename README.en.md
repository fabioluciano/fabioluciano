## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Brazilian Solutions Architect with a passion for building robust and efficient systems in the cloud. My professional journey is driven by a continuous pursuit of optimization, whether in infrastructure, security, or, fundamentally, in the developer experience. With extensive experience in software architecture and infrastructure, I am dedicated to transforming complex challenges into scalable and sustainable solutions.

My expertise ranges from **Cloud Architecture & FinOps** to **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, ensuring teams can innovate with speed and security. I am an enthusiast of **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**, always seeking to improve delivery pipelines and integrate best practices of **DevSecOps & Security** from the design phase. Furthermore, I have a deep interest in **Kubernetes & Containers**, **Observability & SRE**, and **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, areas I consider crucial for the success of any modern **Software Engineering** project. I am a strong advocate for open source and collaboration, actively exploring new tools and approaches to solve problems.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests
Currently, I am immersed in projects and research aimed at improving how we build and deliver software, with a special focus on efficiency and developer experience. My areas of focus include:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing and optimizing cloud infrastructures for maximum performance and cost-effectiveness, with a strong emphasis on financial governance.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Creating environments and tools that empower developers, reducing friction and accelerating the feedback loop.
*   🔄 **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**: Implementing continuous delivery and continuous integration strategies that are agile, reliable, and automated.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security into all phases of the development lifecycle, from design to operation, to build resilient applications.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Developing and maintaining internal platforms that offer self-service tools and services for development teams.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Designing and implementing high-quality, scalable, and easily maintainable software solutions.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Orchestrating applications in containerized environments to ensure scalability, resilience, and portability.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Building systems that provide deep visibility into application and infrastructure behavior, applying Site Reliability Engineering principles.
