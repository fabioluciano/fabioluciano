With a strong foundation in **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**, and **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, my focus is on empowering development teams through well-designed infrastructures and optimized processes.

My expertise extends across **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, **DevSecOps & Security**, **Software Engineering**, **Kubernetes & Containers**, and **Observability & SRE**.
