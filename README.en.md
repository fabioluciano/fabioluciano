## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect passionate about building robust, scalable, and efficient systems. With a career focused on transforming complex challenges into innovative technological solutions, I operate at the intersection of infrastructure, development, and operations. My professional journey is marked by a deep immersion in **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, aiming to optimize costs and performance, and in **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, focusing on tools and processes that empower development teams.

I am an enthusiast of **Modern DevOps & CI/CD** and **DevSecOps & Security**, integrating security practices from the early stages of the software development lifecycle. I have extensive experience in **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, designing and implementing platforms that accelerate value delivery, along with a solid foundation in **Software Engineering** with a focus on scalable solutions. My passion extends to **Kubernetes & Containers**, orchestrating complex environments, and **Observability & SRE**, ensuring the resilience and performance of systems in production.

Proficient in languages like Go, Rust, and TypeScript, and with a particular interest in Cloud Native technologies, automation, and artificial intelligence, I am always exploring the frontiers of innovation. I believe that technology should be an empowering force, and I love collaborating on projects that challenge the status quo and create real impact.
