## 👋 About Me
Fábio Luciano is a seasoned Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer with a passion for optimizing developer experience and building robust, scalable infrastructures. With extensive expertise in **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**, **Kubernetes & Containers**, and **DevSecOps & Security**, he is dedicated to transforming technical complexities into efficient and secure solutions.

His work is driven by the belief that excellence in **Software Engineering** and **Platform Engineering (IDP)** is fundamental to any team's success, always with a keen eye on **Observability & SRE**. Through a holistic approach, Fábio aims not only to implement technologies but also to foster a culture of high performance and collaboration that empowers developers and delivers continuous value.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests
Currently, my efforts and studies are concentrated on the following key areas:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing and optimizing cloud infrastructures for maximum efficiency, scalability, and cost control, ensuring cloud investments yield the highest return.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Creating environments, tools, and processes that empower developers, reducing friction, automating repetitive tasks, and boosting productivity and satisfaction.
*   🔄 **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**: Implementing automated pipelines and continuous practices for rapid, reliable, and secure software delivery, from conception to production.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security into all phases of the development lifecycle, ensuring applications are robust and resilient against threats from initial design.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Building internal platforms that abstract infrastructure complexity, allowing product teams to focus on business logic and innovate more rapidly.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Developing resilient, scalable, and high-quality systems, applying design principles, architectural patterns, and best coding practices.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Orchestrating microservices and containerized applications for high availability, dynamic scalability, and efficient management in distributed environments.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing proactive monitoring solutions, distributed tracing, and reliability practices to ensure the health, performance, and stability of systems in production.
