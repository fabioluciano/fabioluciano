## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer with a passion for building and optimizing robust and efficient development ecosystems. My professional journey is driven by a continuous pursuit of excellence in **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, ensuring that solutions are not only scalable and resilient but also economically viable.

I am dedicated to enhancing **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, creating tools and processes that empower teams to deliver value faster and with less friction. I specialize in implementing **Modern DevOps & CI/CD** practices, automating the software lifecycle from code to production, and integrating security from the outset through **DevSecOps & Security**. My expertise extends to **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, designing and building internal platforms that abstract infrastructure complexity, and **Software Engineering** for distributed and high-performance systems. Furthermore, I have a deep knowledge of **Kubernetes & Containers**, orchestrating applications in complex environments, and **Observability & SRE**, ensuring system health and reliability.

With extensive experience in open-source projects, I actively contribute to the community and constantly seek new ways to innovate. My focus is on translating technical challenges into practical solutions that boost productivity and security in development and production environments.
