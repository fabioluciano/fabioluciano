## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer with extensive experience in building and optimizing development environments and infrastructure. My passion lies in creating robust, scalable systems that, above all, provide an excellent Developer Experience (DevEx). I believe that developer productivity and satisfaction are cornerstones for the success of any product.

With a strong focus on Cloud Architecture & FinOps, I always strive for efficiency and cost optimization without compromising performance or security. I'm an enthusiast of DevOps & Modern CI/CD, applying automation and continuous integration principles to streamline the software development lifecycle. My expertise also extends to DevSecOps & Security, ensuring that security is a concern from the early stages of development.

I am heavily involved in Platform Engineering (IDP), developing internal tools and platforms that empower teams to deliver value autonomously and efficiently. My journey in Software Engineering allows me not only to design but also to build high-quality solutions, with deep knowledge in Kubernetes & Containers for orchestration and scalability. I complete my scope of work with Observability & SRE, ensuring the reliability and resilience of systems in production. I am the creator of the popular `tmux-powerkit`, a tool that reflects my interest in optimizing the developer's workspace.
