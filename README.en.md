## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer passionate about building robust and efficient systems that empower development teams. Based in Brasília, Brazil, I am dedicated to transforming complex challenges into practical solutions, with a strong inclination towards continuous improvement and innovation.

My expertise spans a broad spectrum of critical areas for modern engineering success, including **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**, **DevSecOps & Security**, **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, **Software Engineering**, **Kubernetes & Containers**, and **Observability & SRE**. I am a technical polyglot, proficient in languages such as Go, Rust, TypeScript, and Python, and have a history of contributing to open-source projects, such as the popular `tmux-powerkit`.

I believe that productivity and innovation flourish in environments where workflow is optimized and feedback is constant. My blog reflects this philosophy, exploring topics such as the importance of developer experience and the anatomy of human systems under tension. Currently, I am deeply engaged in exploring and applying **AI Agents** to further enhance efficiency and automation in the software development lifecycle.

*   🤖 **AI Agents & Intelligent Automation**: Exploring the potential of AI agents to automate complex tasks, optimize development workflows, and enhance problem-solving capabilities in software engineering.
