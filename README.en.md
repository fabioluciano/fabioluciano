## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer based in Brasília, Brazil. My passion lies in designing and implementing robust solutions that not only scale and are secure but also empower development teams through an exceptional Developer Experience (DevEx). I firmly believe that developer productivity and satisfaction are cornerstones for the success of any software product.

My professional journey is shaped by deep expertise in **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, where I always seek to optimize costs and resources without compromising performance. I am an enthusiast of **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**, automating the software lifecycle from code to production, and I integrate **DevSecOps & Security** principles from the early stages. I have a particular focus on **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, building tools and platforms that simplify developers' work, alongside a solid foundation in **Software Engineering** to build high-quality systems. I master orchestration with **Kubernetes & Containers** and ensure system stability through **Observability & SRE**.

With 54 public repositories and extensive exploration of topics such as `kubernetes`, `golang`, `devops`, `aws`, and `security`, my work reflects a continuous commitment to innovation and technical excellence. I am a proponent of collaboration and knowledge sharing, always seeking to learn and contribute to the community.
