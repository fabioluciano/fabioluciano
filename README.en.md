## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Brazilian Solutions Architect passionate about optimizing the developer experience and building robust platforms. With a solid foundation in **Software Engineering**, my professional journey focuses on transforming complex challenges into scalable and efficient solutions, ranging from **Cloud Architecture & FinOps** to the implementation of **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**.

I am an open-source enthusiast and active maintainer of projects like `tmux-powerkit`, a popular framework for the tmux status bar, and author of `kubestronaut`, a comprehensive guide to mastering Kubernetes. My curiosity drives me to constantly explore new frontiers, with a strong interest in **DevSecOps & Security**, **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, **Kubernetes & Containers**, and **Observability & SRE**. I firmly believe that an excellent **Developer Experience (DevEx)** is key to high-performing teams.

With over 40 personal repositories and thousands of stars given to open-source projects, I am always seeking to learn and share knowledge. My blog articles cover topics such as productivity, workflow, and the anatomy of Developer Experience, reflecting my commitment to fostering more effective and human-centric development environments.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests
Currently, my energy and attention are focused on the following areas, where I seek to deepen my knowledge and apply innovative solutions:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing scalable and resilient cloud infrastructures with a critical eye on cost optimization and financial governance (FinOps).
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Creating environments and tools that empower developers, improving productivity, satisfaction, and workflow.
*   🔄 **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**: Implementing automated and efficient Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery (CI/CD) pipelines, promoting a DevOps culture for fast and secure deliveries.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security practices throughout all phases of the software development lifecycle, from design to operation, to build inherently secure systems.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Developing and maintaining Internal Developer Platforms (IDPs) that abstract infrastructure complexity, allowing product teams to focus on business logic.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Applying software design principles, architectural patterns, and best practices to build robust, maintainable, and high-performance systems.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Orchestrating containerized applications with Kubernetes, mastering aspects of deployment, scalability, management, and operation of cloud-native workloads.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing monitoring, logging, and tracing solutions to ensure complete visibility into systems, applying Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) principles to maintain reliability and performance.
