## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer based in Brasília, Brazil, passionate about building robust, scalable, and secure systems. My professional journey is marked by a relentless pursuit of optimization, whether in cloud architecture or in enhancing the daily developer experience. I believe that technical excellence goes hand-in-hand with creating environments that empower teams to innovate and deliver value efficiently.

My expertise spans a broad and critical spectrum of modern engineering: **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, where I focus on cost optimization and performance in cloud environments; **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, aiming to simplify and improve developer workflows; **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**, implementing cutting-edge automation and continuous delivery pipelines; **DevSecOps & Security**, integrating security practices from the earliest stages of development; **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, building internal tools and platforms that accelerate the software lifecycle; **Software Engineering**, developing high-quality solutions; **Kubernetes & Containers**, mastering container orchestration and cloud-native ecosystems; and **Observability & SRE**, ensuring proactive monitoring and reliability of systems.

As a maintainer of successful open-source projects, such as `tmux-powerkit`, and an author of articles on productivity and DevEx, I constantly seek to share knowledge and contribute to the community. My versatility in languages like Go, Rust, TypeScript, and Python allows me to tackle complex challenges with the right tool for the job, always with a strategic eye for innovation and operational efficiency.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests
Currently, I dedicate my energy and research to the following areas, aiming to deepen my knowledge and apply best practices in each:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing and optimizing cloud infrastructures for maximum scalability, resilience, and cost efficiency, with a focus on platforms like AWS and Cloudflare.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Creating environments and tools that make the development process more fluid, enjoyable, and productive, minimizing friction and accelerating feedback.
*   🔄 **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**: Implementing advanced automation strategies for continuous integration, continuous delivery, and continuous deployment, utilizing tools like ArgoCD, FluxCD, and Helm.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security as a fundamental pillar at all stages of the development lifecycle, from design to operation, ensuring data and system protection.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Building and maintaining internal platforms that provide developers with a self-service suite of tools and services, accelerating value delivery and standardizing operations.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Developing high-quality, performant, and maintainable software, with a focus on clean design principles, robust architectures, and effective coding practices.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Mastering container orchestration, Kubernetes cluster management, and cloud-native ecosystems for distributed and scalable applications.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing monitoring, logging, and tracing solutions with tools like Prometheus and Grafana, and applying Site Reliability Engineering principles to ensure high availability and performance of systems.
