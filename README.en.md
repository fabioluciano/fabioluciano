Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect with a profound passion for optimizing the developer journey and building robust platforms. My professional path is marked by an unceasing pursuit of efficiency and innovation, operating at the intersection of infrastructure, development, and operations. With a strong focus on **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, and **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, I help teams overcome complex challenges and deliver value more quickly and securely.

Through open source contributions, articles, and discussions, I seek to share knowledge and inspire the next generation of engineers.
