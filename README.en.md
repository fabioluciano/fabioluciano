## 👋 About Me

Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect with a passion for building robust, scalable systems that, above all, provide an exceptional Developer Experience (DevEx). With a solid foundation in **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, my focus is on optimizing infrastructures and processes to ensure not only performance and security but also cost efficiency and sustainability.

My professional journey has led me to delve into **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**, **DevSecOps & Security**, and **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, always striving to create environments where software development is fluid and frictionless. I'm an enthusiast of **Kubernetes & Containers**, leveraging these technologies to orchestrate complex applications, and an advocate for **Observability & SRE** to maintain resilient and transparent systems. Furthermore, my expertise in **Software Engineering** allows me to transition between high-level design and practical implementation, always with a keen eye for code quality and innovation.

I am an active developer in the Go and Rust ecosystem, with a particular interest in CLI tools and distributed systems. I contribute to open-source projects, such as the popular `tmux-powerkit`, and am the author of the `kubestronaut` guide for Kubernetes certifications. I believe that the key to technological success lies in combining technical excellence with a culture that values the developer's experience, and that's what I aim to promote in every project.

*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Building and maintaining Internal Developer Platforms (IDPs) that empower engineering teams with self-service tools and services, standardizing environments, and accelerating value delivery.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Applying robust software design principles and architectural patterns to create high-quality, maintainable, and scalable solutions, with a focus on languages like Go, Rust, and TypeScript.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Mastering container orchestration with Kubernetes for deploying, scaling, and managing distributed applications, also exploring solutions like vCluster for development environments.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing monitoring, logging, and tracing strategies to ensure full visibility into systems, applying Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) principles to maintain high availability and reliability.

## 🌱 Currently Learning

I am constantly exploring new technologies and concepts to enhance my skills and stay ahead in the tech landscape. My current focus includes:

*   🤖 **AI-Powered Development & RAG**: Diving into tools like `claude-context` to integrate artificial intelligence into code search and generation, exploring Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and vector databases to optimize engineering workflows.
*   🛠️ **Cloud Local Development Tools**: Deepening my understanding of local AWS emulator ecosystems, such as `floci`, to create more efficient and replicable development environments, reducing dependencies on actual cloud environments during development.
*   📈 **SLOs and Advanced Observability**: Exploring Service Level Objectives (SLOs) generators like `sloth` for Prometheus, enhancing the definition and monitoring of system reliability and performance goals.
*   🦀 **Network Engineering with Rust**: Investigating the use of Rust to build high-performance network tools, such as reverse proxies (`rustunnel`), demonstrating interest in the security and efficiency that the language offers for infrastructure.
*   🖥️ **CLI Utilities & Productivity Tools**: Exploring Go-based tools like `sonar` for inspecting local services and `lazycut` for terminal-based video editing, always seeking to optimize workflow and daily productivity.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes in Development Environments**: Experimenting with solutions like `vCluster in Docker (vind)` to create isolated and ephemeral Kubernetes environments for development and testing, facilitating prototyping and testing of containerized applications.
