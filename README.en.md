## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect passionate about building robust systems and optimizing the developer experience. With a career focused on the intersection of technology and efficiency, I'm dedicated to transforming complex challenges into scalable and secure solutions. My expertise spans from **Cloud Architecture & FinOps** to **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, with a strong commitment to driving **Developer Experience (DevEx)**.

Throughout my journey, I've delved deeply into **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**, **DevSecOps & Security**, and environment orchestration with **Kubernetes & Containers**. I'm an enthusiast of high-quality **Software Engineering** and constantly seek to improve **Observability & SRE** to ensure application stability and performance. My passion for communities and open-source projects is evident in initiatives like `tmux-powerkit` and the `kubestronaut` guide, where I aim to share knowledge and tools that empower developers.

My approach goes beyond code, focusing on how teams interact with technology to create value sustainably. I believe that productivity is a human system under tension, and for this reason, I dedicate myself to exploring the flow, feedback, and the unseen load, as discussed on my blog.
