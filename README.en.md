## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect passionate about optimizing the developer journey and building robust, scalable platforms. With a career focused on the intersection of technology and efficiency, I'm dedicated to creating environments where engineering teams can thrive, delivering value with agility and security.

My expertise spans **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, ensuring solutions are not only functional but also cost-efficient; enhancing **Developer Experience (DevEx)** to maximize productivity; and implementing **DevOps & Modern CI/CD** practices for continuous automation. I also have deep knowledge of **DevSecOps & Security**, integrating security from design, and **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, building infrastructures that empower developers.

I have a special passion for **Software Engineering**, focusing on distributed systems and microservices architectures, mastering the world of **Kubernetes & Containers** for orchestration and scalability. My pursuit of excellence extends to **Observability & SRE**, ensuring systems are resilient and well-monitored. I'm an active advocate for Open Source, the creator of `tmux-powerkit`, and a continuous learner, always exploring the frontiers of technology.
