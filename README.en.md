## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect passionate about building robust systems and, above all, enhancing the experience of those who develop them. With a career focused on the intersection of infrastructure, software engineering, and culture, I constantly strive to optimize workflows and empower teams.

My expertise spans from **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, ensuring scalable and cost-efficient solutions, to **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, where I lay the groundwork for developers to innovate autonomously. I am an enthusiast of **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**, integrating practices that accelerate value delivery, and a staunch advocate for **DevSecOps & Security**, ensuring security is intrinsic to the development process.

With over 50 public repositories, including widely used projects like `tmux-powerkit`, and a history of active contributions in discussions and code reviews, I demonstrate my commitment to the community and continuous improvement. My journey is driven by the pursuit of excellence in **Software Engineering**, mastery in **Kubernetes & Containers**, and the implementation of **Observability & SRE** for resilient systems.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests
Currently, I dedicate my energy and research to the following areas, always seeking to innovate and apply best practices:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing and optimizing cloud infrastructures (AWS, GCP, Azure) with a focus on scalability, resilience, and cost control, ensuring technology serves business objectives sustainably.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Creating environments, tools, and processes that empower developers, simplifying complex workflows, reducing friction, and increasing team productivity and satisfaction.
*   🔄 **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**: Implementing end-to-end automated pipelines and continuous delivery practices, accelerating the software development lifecycle with quality, security, and reliability.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security as a fundamental pillar at every stage of the development cycle, from design to operation, ensuring robust systems protected against threats.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Developing and maintaining Internal Developer Platforms (IDPs) that abstract infrastructure complexity, allowing engineering teams to focus on business logic.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Applying design and coding principles to build robust, maintainable, and high-performance systems, with a focus on distributed architectures and microservices.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Orchestrating applications in containerized environments, exploring the elasticity, efficiency, and resilience of Kubernetes for large-scale deployments.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Implementing monitoring, tracing, and logging solutions, and applying Site Reliability Engineering practices to ensure the health, performance, and reliability of complex distributed systems.

## 🌱 Currently Learning
My passion for technology keeps me in a constant state of learning. Lately, I've been actively exploring:

*   **Advanced Observability & SLOs**: Deepening my understanding of tools like `sloth` for automated Service Level Objective (SLO) generation and management with Prometheus, aiming for even more proactive and data-driven SRE.
