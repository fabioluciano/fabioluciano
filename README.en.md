## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer passionate about creating efficient and secure development environments. My professional journey is marked by a continuous pursuit of optimization, from infrastructure to developer experience. With a strategic focus on **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, I ensure that solutions not only scale but are also financially sustainable.

I am dedicated to enhancing **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, understanding that optimized workflows and effective tooling are crucial for team productivity and satisfaction. I am an enthusiast of **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**, implementing pipelines that automate and accelerate the delivery cycle, always with a focus on **DevSecOps & Security** to protect systems from design onwards. My expertise extends to **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, where I build and maintain platforms that empower teams to deliver value autonomously.

With a solid foundation in **Software Engineering**, I apply best practices to develop robust and scalable systems, using **Kubernetes & Containers** as pillars for orchestration and deployment. I believe visibility is power, which is why I invest in **Observability & SRE** to ensure the continuous reliability and performance of applications. My work aims not only to build but also to empower, document, and optimize, transforming complex challenges into elegant and functional solutions.
