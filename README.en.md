## 👋 About Me
As a Cloud Solutions Architect, Fábio Luciano is dedicated to building development ecosystems that not only function but thrive. His passion lies in optimizing every stage of the software lifecycle, from architectural design to delivery and operation, with a vision that integrates performance, security, and the developer experience.

With deep expertise in **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, he designs scalable and efficient solutions, ensuring that infrastructure aligns with business objectives and cost control. His vision extends to **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, where he strives to eliminate friction and empower teams with tools and processes that boost productivity. Fábio is a tireless advocate for modern **DevOps & Modern CI/CD** practices, streamlining continuous and secure delivery pipelines.

His work in **DevSecOps & Security** ensures that security is a pillar, not an afterthought. At the forefront of **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, Fábio designs and implements internal platforms that abstract infrastructure complexity. His solid foundation in **Software Engineering** complements this vision, enabling the construction of resilient and high-performance systems. Furthermore, he masters **Kubernetes & Containers** for orchestration and scale, and applies **Observability & SRE** principles to guarantee continuous system reliability and availability. His approach is holistic, believing that technical excellence must go hand in hand with optimizing human processes and engineering culture.
