## 👋 About Me

I'm Fábio Luciano, a Brazilian Solutions Architect passionate about building robust, scalable, and, above all, exceptional Developer Experience (DevEx) systems. My professional journey is marked by a relentless pursuit of optimization, whether in designing cloud solutions, automating DevOps pipelines, or creating platforms that empower developers. With a solid foundation in **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**, and **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, I am dedicated to transforming complex challenges into efficient and high-impact solutions.

My expertise spans from orchestrating microservices with **Kubernetes & Containers** to implementing **DevSecOps & Security** practices, ensuring that innovation goes hand-in-hand with resilience and compliance. I believe that quality **Software Engineering** is the cornerstone of any successful system, and **Observability & SRE** are crucial for maintaining health and performance in production environments. I am an active advocate for Developer Experience, continuously exploring how tools and processes can enhance developer workflows and team satisfaction, as detailed in my recent articles.

With extensive experience in languages like Go, Rust, TypeScript, and Python, and a track record of contributions to open-source projects like `tmux-powerkit` and `kubestronaut`, I am always exploring new technological frontiers. My focus is on delivering value through well-designed architectures and an engineering culture that values collaboration and continuous improvement.

## 🎯 Current Focus & Interests

Currently, my activities and research are concentrated on the following key areas, aiming to drive innovation and efficiency in software development:

*   ☁️ **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**: Designing resilient and scalable cloud infrastructures, with a strong emphasis on cost optimization and financial governance (FinOps) to maximize return on investment.
*   👨‍💻 **Developer Experience (DevEx)**: Focused on enhancing the developer's work environment and tools, reducing friction, accelerating feedback cycles, and increasing team satisfaction and productivity.
*   🔄 **Modern DevOps & CI/CD**: Implementing and refining Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery (CI/CD) practices that automate the software lifecycle, from code to production, with agility and security.
*   🔐 **DevSecOps & Security**: Integrating security as an intrinsic part of every development and operation stage, ensuring applications are secure by design and by default, through automation and rigorous policies.
*   🏗️ **Platform Engineering (IDP)**: Building and maintaining Internal Developer Platforms (IDPs) that abstract infrastructure complexity, allowing developers to focus on business logic.
*   ⚙️ **Software Engineering**: Applying software design principles, architectural patterns, and best practices to build high-quality, maintainable, and performant systems.
*   ☸️ **Kubernetes & Containers**: Mastering container orchestration with Kubernetes for deploying, scaling, and managing distributed applications, as well as exploring advanced containerization technologies.
*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Developing robust monitoring, logging, and tracing systems to ensure complete visibility into application health and applying Site Reliability Engineering principles to maintain high reliability.
