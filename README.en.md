## 👋 About Me
As a Solutions Architect, I dedicate my career to building bridges between business vision and technical excellence, with a special focus on optimizing Developer Experience (DevEx) and driving cloud innovation. My professional journey is marked by a passion for designing and implementing robust, scalable, and secure architectures, always striving to enhance developer workflows and operational efficiency.

I possess deep expertise in **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, ensuring that solutions not only meet technical needs but are also financially sustainable. I am an enthusiast of **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**, applying automation and continuous delivery principles to accelerate the software development lifecycle. My work extends to **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, creating tools and infrastructures that empower teams to develop and operate with greater autonomy and security.

With a solid foundation in **Software Engineering**, I actively explore **Kubernetes & Containers** for workload orchestration and **Observability & SRE** to maintain resilient and performant systems. Security is a constant priority, and my experience in **DevSecOps & Security** ensures that security practices are integrated from the start of the development cycle. In addition to contributing to open-source projects like `tmux-powerkit`, I am always exploring new technological frontiers, such as the emerging field of AI Agents, to shape the future of development and operations.
