## 👋 About Me
Hello! I'm Fábio Luciano, a Solutions Architect and Platform Engineer passionate about transforming complex challenges into efficient and scalable solutions. Based in Brasília, Brazil, I am dedicated to optimizing the developer experience (DevEx) and building robust infrastructures in cloud-native environments. My professional journey is marked by a continuous pursuit of innovation and empowering teams through cutting-edge technologies.

My expertise covers a comprehensive range of critical areas for modern software development: from **Cloud Architecture & FinOps**, ensuring efficiency and cost control, and **Platform Engineering (IDP)**, building the foundation for productivity, to **Developer Experience (DevEx)**, simplifying engineers' daily work. I am also an enthusiast of **DevOps & Modern CI/CD**, implementing continuous delivery workflows, and **DevSecOps & Security**, integrating security at every stage of the lifecycle. My passion extends to high-quality **Software Engineering**, orchestration with **Kubernetes & Containers**, and reliability through **Observability & SRE**.

With a history of over 50 public repositories and extensive technology exploration, I am an active knowledge curator and a contributor to the open-source ecosystem, as evidenced by projects like `tmux-powerkit` and `kubestronaut`. Beyond code, I share insights and experiences on my blog, acting as a thought leader who constantly seeks to elevate the standard of software engineering.

*   📊 **Observability & SRE**: Developing comprehensive monitoring, logging, and tracing strategies to gain deep visibility into system behavior, ensuring high availability and performance.
